Cookies and Cream Fudge

Some delicious fudge

Ingredients

  • White chocolate
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Oreos

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine 510g of white chocolate and 400g of condensed milk
  2. Heat in the microwave in increments of 15 seconds until fully melted
  3. Add in your chopped or crushed cookies and stir until combined
  4. Pour fudge mixture into a 20x20cm baking tin lined with parchment paper
  5. Chill for 2 hours or until fudge is firm
  6. Cut into bite-sized pieces
  7. Enjoy!
