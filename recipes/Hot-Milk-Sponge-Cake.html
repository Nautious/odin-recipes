Hot Milk Sponge Cake

Some delicious hot milk cake!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tsp. baking power
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Sift flower, baking powder and salt together
  3. In a bowl, mix your milk and butter and heat till melted - keep hot
  4. Beat eggs till thick and lemon-coloured
  5. Add sugar and mix at steady pace for 4-5 minutes
  6. Add rest of dry ingredients, stir till blended
  7. Add in hot milk mixture and vanilla, blend well
  8. Pour into greased 9x9x2" pan
  9. Cook for 25-30 minutess
  10. Take out and let cool
  11. Cut into smaller pieces
  12. (optional) add a swirl of whip cream and fruit ontop
  13. Enjoy!
